Pig game

This project was created through a series of coding challenges in a JavaScript course. PigGame is a 2 player game: Roll the dice and stack up points to reach 100 points before the opponent. The player is switched when the current player chooses to hold and collects points or when a dice with 1 is rolled. In the latter case, all unheld points are forfeited.
